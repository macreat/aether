What you are describing is evolving from a **research pipeline** into a **general Agent Engineering Platform**.

The research pipeline becomes only one of many workflows.

The real product becomes:

> A local-first multi-agent operating environment where users can upload documents, repositories, ideas, papers, requirements, or research topics, and dynamically assemble specialized agents to analyze, generate, audit, research, code, and continuously update project artifacts.
